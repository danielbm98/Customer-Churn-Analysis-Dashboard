## 🎯 Business Objective

Build an interactive analytics dashboard that enables users to explore customer churn for a telecom company by analyzing churn rate, customer demographics, account characteristics, payment behavior, and service usage. The dashboard supports retention strategy and customer experience analysis through interactive filtering across multiple dimensions.

---

## ❓ Business Questions Answered

This dashboard helps answer questions such as:

- What is the overall customer churn rate?
- Which churn reasons and categories drive customer loss the most?
- How does churn vary by age group and account tenure?
- Is there a relationship between monthly charges and churn?
- Which payment methods and contract types have the highest churn rates?
- How does account tenure relate to churn across different payment/contract combinations?
- Do extra data and international charges influence churn?
- Does data usage or having an unlimited plan affect churn likelihood?
- How do customer support calls relate to churn?
- How does churn vary geographically by state?

---

## 🚀 Dashboard Features

- Interactive filtering by payment method, contract type, internet plan, and unlimited data plan
- KPI cards for churn rate, customer count, and total/average customer service calls
- Churn reason and churn category breakdowns
- Age and account-length group analysis with combo charts (customers vs. churn rate)
- Scatter plots comparing tenure, monthly charges, and extra charges against churn status
- Geographic distribution of churn by state
- Multi-page layout for focused, drill-down analysis

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators

| KPI | Description |
|---|---|
| **Churn Rate** | Percentage of customers who canceled service |
| **Number of Customers** | Total customer count in the current filter context |
| **Churned Customers** | Total count of customers who churned |
| **Avg Customer Service Calls** | Average number of support calls per customer |
| **Avg Extra Data / Int'l Charges** | Average additional charges incurred by customers |

## 📊 Dashboard Walkthrough

### Overview
High-level summary of churn, including churn rate, customer count, churned customers, top churn reasons, churn rate by category, and geographic distribution.

### Age Groups
Analyzes churn and customer volume across age bins and account-length groups, comparing customer counts, monthly charges, and churn rate.

### Payment and Contract
Compares churn and account tenure across payment methods and contract types (Category and Type), with filters for both dimensions.

### Extra Charges
Examines how data usage, unlimited data plans, and average extra data/international charges relate to churn rate and churn status.

### Insights
Explores customer support call patterns by state and churn status alongside extra charges, tying service experience to churn outcomes.

## 🗂️ Data Source

This project uses the **Telecom Customer Churn** dataset from [Maven Analytics' Data Playground](https://mavenanalytics.io/data-playground/telecom-customer-churn) — customer data for a fictional telecom company ("Databel") providing phone and internet services to customers in California.

---

## 📈 Business Value

This dashboard turns raw customer account and service data into actionable retention insights, helping stakeholders understand who is churning, why, and under what conditions. It demonstrates how business intelligence tools can support customer experience strategy and proactive retention efforts.
